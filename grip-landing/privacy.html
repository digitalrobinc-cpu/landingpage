Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

At GRIP, accessible from our mobile application and website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GRIP and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you register an account, customize your motorsports interest profile, manage your garage, book trackdays, or participate in convoy drives:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password, and contact details.
  • Garage & Profile Data: Vehicle specifications, engine maps, modifications list, build logs, and setup details.
  • Location & Telemetry Data: Real-time GPS location coordinates (primarily for Convoy Planning and tracking), lap times, and telemetry sensor data.
  • Transaction Data: Payment details when booking track slots or buying products in the marketplace.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our mobile application and landing page.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our platform experiences tailored specifically to your garage.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our application to build better features.
  • Facilitate real-time location mapping during group convoy planning.
  • Process payments for trackdays and marketplace transactions.
  • Communicate with you for customer support, product updates, and beta access.

3. Location Sharing

Real-time location sharing is a core component of the Convoy Planning feature. Your location coordinates are only broadcasted to members of your active convoy group and are not shared publicly or with third-party advertising services.

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@grip-app.co.
