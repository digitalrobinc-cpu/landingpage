About the Founder

R

Robin

Founder & Chief Driver, GRIP

Robin's passion for motorsports isn't just a hobby — it's the foundation of GRIP. As a lifelong trackday racer, offroad explorer, and mechanic, Robin spent years navigating the fragmented landscape of the global motorsport community.

Whether he was chasing lap times on circuit asphalt in his sports car, preparing trail rigs for overland expeditions, or maintaining street-legal performance bikes, one core problem remained constant: the community was scattered across outdated web forums, generic social networking apps, and clunky scheduling sheets.

"I created GRIP because I wanted one unified, premium platform built specifically for the way we live. We shouldn't have to switch between five different apps just to organize a convoy, trade specialized racing parts, list build specs, or book a local track day."

The GRIP Mission

Under Robin's leadership, GRIP was engineered to consolidate the entire motorsport lifestyle. By connecting trackday organisers directly to circuit racers, facilitating rugged trail routes for offroad explorers, and managing garage telemetry databases in one sleek super app, GRIP is built to serve every driver on two wheels or four wheels.

Today, Robin leads a dedicated product team focused on building high-performance features that give drivers exactly what they need: precision, speed, and real connections on the grid.
